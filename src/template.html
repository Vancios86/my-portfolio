About me

I am a self-taught Web Developer, fascinated by the art of code

I wasn't always a techie, for example, the photo next to this text was taken a few years ago while I was working as a sommelier in a Michelin Star restaurant and living in London. As well I administered a private pool in high school, worked for a bank while in uni followed by a job for a credit brokerage company specialized in banking products. I even sustained myself financially activating as a professional poker player on the UK live poker scene for a couple of years until the pandemic started. This is when I turned my focus towards Web Development and started studying and practicing on my own day in day out. Started small, one step at a time, failed a lot, then succeeded, then failed again but never looked back. Today, after many months and multiple specialized courses, a 4 digit number of hours spent studying and practicing, plus an internship as a Frontend Developer at a prestigious company, I know that my future and my passion for coding will go hand in hand.
