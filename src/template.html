About me

Self-taught Web Developer, passionate about Information Technology, Graphic Design and User Experience.

I wasn't always a techie, for example, the photo next to this text was taken a few years ago while I was a sommelier in a Michelin Star restaurant and living in London. As well I administered a public pool in high school, worked for a bank while in uni followed by a job for a credit brokerage company specialized on banking products, and even sustained myself financially activating as a professional poker player on the UK live poker scene for a couple of years, until the pandemic closed all the poker rooms and locked us inside the house. This is when I turned my focus towards Web Development and started studying and practicing on my own day in day out. Started small, one step at a time, failed a lot, then succeeded, then failed again but never looked back. Meanwhile, I got proficient in using HTML5, CSS and SCSS, skilled in modern JavaScript, practiced with VueJS and ReactJS frameworks, was active as a WordPress freelancer and built few nice websites for my clients, obtained and concluded an internship for a cool company where I've learned a lot and understood that I want coding to be part of my future. As well I studied Backend basics to integrate myself better within the teams I will be part of in the future projects. I don't know where this journey will take me, but I'm excited to discover it day by day.
