About me

Self-taught Web Developer, passionate about Information Technology, Graphic Design and User Experience.

I wasn't always a techie, for example, the photo next to this text was taken a few years ago while working as a sommelier in a Michelin Star restaurant in London. As well I administered a public pool in high school, worked for a housing bank while in uni, and sustained myself financially playing poker across various cardrooms for few years in the attempt to find my calling...it finally struck me when I got myself enrolled in a Full-Stack Development course on my e-learning platform, and started studying and practicing day in day out. After getting finished the complex Full-Stack course, I've decided to channel my efforts towards Front-End side of Web Developing until I get really good at it, and then shifting to the Back-End. Meanwhile, I got skilled in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, practiced in using React library, became versatile in responsive design while possessing Back-End basics to integrate myself better within the team from future projects. As well I became proficient in WordPress because it's fun and my services are listed on a professional website in case you want a cool blog or e-commerce website. I've noticed that my analytical skills and creative problem solving improved and continue to do so. I don't know where this journey will take me, but I'm excited to discover it a little at a time.
